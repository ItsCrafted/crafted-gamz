About Crafted Gamz

The Elusion Family

Elusion is a quick, one-stop-shop offering safe access to games, apps, media, and web utilities -- ad-free and at no cost. Its design is centered on speed, privacy, and reliability, connecting users in an open, ever-expanding community.

Our Shared Values

  • Security and privacy as a priority
  • Fast, reliable access with minimal lag
  • A welcoming, helpful, and engaging community
  • Constantly updating and improving our services

Legacy and Growth

The Elusion family includes sites such as Tide, Evadion, Acorn, Crafted Gamz (us :D), Auraa, MizzX, and Moonlight Dispenser. As we continue to grow and evolve, we welcome new members and projects.

We invite everyone who shares our passion for open and creative internet spaces to join our Discord community and be a part of this journey.
